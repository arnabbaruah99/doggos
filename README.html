Know more about dog breeds

  • Lhasa Apso

    The Lhasa Apso is a small, long-haired breed originally from Tibet. They are known for their distinct, luxurious coat and are generally friendly, playful, and loyal companions. They are suitable for apartment living and make great watchdogs.

  • Golden Retriever

    The Golden Retriever is a friendly, intelligent, and devoted dog breed. They are known for their golden-colored, water-repellent coat. They are great family pets and are popular for their obedience and agility.

  • Bulldog

    The Bulldog is a medium-sized dog breed known for its wrinkled face and distinctive pushed-in nose. They have a gentle and friendly disposition, making them great companions for families. Bulldogs are generally calm and courageous dogs.

  • German Shepherd

    The German Shepherd is a breed known for its intelligence, strength, and versatility. They are often used as working dogs in various roles, including search and rescue, police work, and assistance to people with disabilities. They are loyal and make excellent guard dogs.

  • Poodle

    The Poodle is a highly intelligent and elegant dog breed known for its curly, hypoallergenic coat. They come in three different sizes: standard, miniature, and toy. Poodles are skillful in many dog sports and activities and are known for their agility and trainability.

  • Beagle

    The Beagle is a small to medium-sized dog breed known for its friendly, curious, and merry personality. They are popular for their keen sense of smell and are often used as detection dogs for various purposes. Beagles are generally good-natured and make excellent family pets.

  • Labrador Retriever

    The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds known for its friendly and outgoing nature. They are often used as working dogs in roles such as assistance dogs, therapy dogs, and search and rescue dogs. Labradors are highly trainable and make wonderful family companions.

  • Siberian Husky

    The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working dog breed known for its thick double coat and striking blue or multi-colored eyes. They are known for their endurance, strong-willed nature, and ability to work in harsh cold climates. Siberian Huskies are energetic, mischievous, and sociable dogs.

  • Dachshund

    The Dachshund, also known as the "wiener dog," is a small dog breed with a long body and short legs. They come in three coat varieties: smooth, wirehaired, and longhaired. Dachshunds are brave, clever, and playful, and they make affectionate and entertaining family pets.

  • Boxer

    The Boxer is a medium to large-sized dog breed known for its strong, muscular build and distinctive square-shaped head. They are playful, energetic, and friendly, making them excellent companions for families. Boxers are known for their loyalty and protective nature, making them great guard dogs.

  • Rottweiler

    The Rottweiler is a powerful and robust dog breed known for its strength and protective instincts. They are often used as working dogs in roles such as police dogs, guard dogs, and search and rescue dogs. Rottweilers are loyal, confident, and courageous, making them excellent guardians and companions.

  • Pug

    The Pug is a small dog breed known for its wrinkled face and curled tail. They have a charming and mischievous personality, and they are affectionate and playful with their families. Pugs are suitable for apartment living and are known for their loving and social nature, making them excellent companion dogs.

  • Great Dane

    The Great Dane is a giant dog breed known for its impressive size and elegant appearance. Despite their large stature, they are known for their gentle and friendly nature. Great Danes are patient, dependable, and good-natured, making them affectionate and devoted companions for families.
